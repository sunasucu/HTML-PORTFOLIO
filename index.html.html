🌱 My Journey:

  • From Banking to Teaching, and Now into Tech
    Throughout my life, I’ve always been passionate about learning and personal growth. My journey has taken me through very different paths — from working as a customer representative at a bank in Turkey, to teaching English in India and Vietnam, and now stepping into the world of software development.
  • 💼 From Corporate Life to the Classroom
    After graduating from university, I began my career as a customer representative in a bank. It taught me how to communicate effectively, solve problems under pressure, and manage responsibilities in a fast-paced environment. But over time, I realized something was missing. Deep down, I had a strong desire to share knowledge, to teach, and to explore new cultures.
  • 🌏 A New Beginning as an English Teacher
    That inner calling led me to become an English teacher. I taught myself English through my own methods and later earned my TESOL certificate. I started teaching in India and now continue to teach in Vietnam. I've had the chance to work with hundreds of students, each one offering a new perspective. Teaching, for me, became more than just a profession — it was a way to connect, inspire, and build bridges between cultures.
  • 👩‍💻 Stepping into the World of Code
    While teaching, my interest in technology grew stronger. I’ve always loved solving problems, and coding offered the perfect challenge. I began dedicating my evenings and weekends to learning programming. Today, I’m actively studying software development, data science, and web technologies.
  • I'm learning languages like Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and regularly practicing on platforms like CodeWars and HackerRank. For me, learning to code isn’t just about changing careers — it's a mindset shift that empowers me to build, create, and think differently
